(Our Story)

a testament to the spirit of Pioneer Square

Developed by Urban Villagesand operated by Aparium Hotel Groupand architecturally designed by The Miller Hull Partnershipwith interiors by Curioso, the independent lifestyle hotel promises a unique escape in the Pacific Northwest. Hotel Westland will highlight historic character maintained from the building’s 1907 construction, modern guest rooms and amenities, Pioneer Square’s first rooftop bar, and a signature restaurant fueled by an authentic connection to community and Seattle’s deep-rooted history.

( The Neighborhood )

WHERE PAST AND PRESENT CONVERGE

Hotel Westland will serve as anchor of the transformative RailSpur micro district, which has become a cultural nexus where art, music, history and community converge. As you explore the neighborhood, you’ll discover the hotel’s commitment to fostering the arts-centric energy that defines the area. From the cobblestone streets to the soaring structures that tell tales of Seattle's history, we invite you to step into a living narrative of the cityʻs evolution.
